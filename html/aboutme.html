Early College Program





In 2020, right during the COVID-19 Pandemic, I heard about a program called the Early College Program. This program would allow me to attend college my Junior and Senior years of high school and earn my Associates of Arts in Business for free.

I took the opportunity and joined the program. This was the best decision I could have made. I am now 18 and a Junior at Brigham Young University because I was willing to take a chance.



This program has allowed me to excel in school. Listed below are the high schools I attended as well as the university where I earned my degree, Cumberland University.

  1. Cumberland University
      2. Associates of Arts in Business
      4.0 GPA, Summa Cum Laude

  2. Green Hill High School and Mount Juliet High School
      3. High School Diploma
      4.7 GPA, Summa Cum Laude
